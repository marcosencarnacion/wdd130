Colombia

Colombia, located in the northwestern part of South America is a country of remarkable diversity and beauty. It is the fifth largest country in Latin America and home of the world's second largest population of Spanish-speaking people. Colombia boasts stunning landscapes ranging from the lush Amazon rainforest and towering Andes mountains to the sparkling Caribbean and Pacific coastlines. Colombia is famous for its rich coffee plantations, colorful colonial towns like Cartagena, and a lively cultural heritage expressed through music, dance, and festivals. Its biodiversity is one of the highest in the world, making it a paradise for nature lovers. Despite past challenges, Colombia today is a thriving destination celebrated for its warmth, resilience, and hospitality. -Britannica
